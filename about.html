Our Story

The journey behind Savory Bites Restaurant

Where It All Began

Founded in 2010 by acclaimed chef Michael Anderson, Savory Bites started as a small caf√© with a big dream: to serve exceptional food that celebrates both tradition and innovation.

Growing up in a family that valued home-cooked meals, Michael learned to appreciate the power of food to bring people together. After training at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and working at several Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, he returned home with a vision.

His philosophy was simple yet ambitious: source the finest ingredients locally, prepare them with respect and creativity, and serve them in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
